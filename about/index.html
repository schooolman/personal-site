I’ve made it to four of the seven continents so far, I plan on making it to all seven.

My biggest guilty pleasure is my love of videogames and custom built keyboards, (yes, like the ones you type on). However, I try to keep physically active by bicycling frequently and spending my weekends outdoors.

The best advice I have ever received is to learn something new everyday, I do my best to stick by that.
